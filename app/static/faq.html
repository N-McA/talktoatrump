FAQ

Why doesn't this work for me?

This site uses some advanced web technology to connect you directly to another person. Unfortunately it doesn't always work. You should try again on Firefox or Chrome, and perhaps wait a bit longer. (Also it was written in a couple of hours, so really we've no idea about edge case bugs.)

Why is this a thing?

Donald Trump has been elected president of the USA. Unfortunately, there has been a lot of divisive rhetoric from both sides of the political spectrum. Hopefully this site will serve as a reminder that the people on both sides are just people.

Why is there no texting?

Seeing a face is a better reminder of our common humanity. WebRTC video is fun.
